Flashlights

There was something, in the flashlights,
and like a pack of vultures they circled
through the night. They would cheer
and cry, with their hands up high – it
could have been for me... just imagine
that.
You want something? Something special? And like a pack of vultures, you would have torn it down. Still they get it all! Their face up on the wall. It should have been for me... just remember that!
I wanna been rich and then some. Smooth and loud. I wanna go elsewhere, you can stay and hold the door. I wanna be loved and cherished, like your favourite book – I want it all right here, right now, ah ah ah aaah.
